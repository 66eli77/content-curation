Use this page to render your code for your handlebars templates as you develop!

After your template is finished, create a new .handlebars file, copy your code into it, and place it in the respective hbtemplates folder. There's a sample in the hbtemplates folder.

Make all of your styles inside of styles.less. At this stage, the styling does not have to completely look like the mockups, so don't worry about perfection (yet) :)
